EN

My name is Gonçalo Lima, I am 21 years old and I study Software Engineering at ESTG-IPVC (Polytechnic Institute of Viana do Castelo). I consider myself an enthusiastic of new technologies and nature related sports, more specifically canoeing.

I decided to choose this course because it has always been a topic that captured my interest and it has ever since allowed me to enter the "world" of programming, through the languages and methodologies that are taught in class. With that in mind, over my academic course, I have been acquiring several skills (both practical and cognitive) that are essential to my development and which enable me to practice the profession of Software Engineer. Thus, with the aid of this acquired knowledge I am still able to learn new languages, after school hours, in a self-taught way.

Before this course I was a member of an online forum which focused on working with css and html. I have also created many blog of my own during high school, which allowed me to test many techniques that proved to me important.

During the course I learned C, Java, PHP, NodeJS, among others technologies.

Apart from that, I have developed a site that has been very successful and all it's construction, maintenance and dedication for it to grow has lead me to be interested on exploring Search Engine Optimization, in order to reach the first places on google.

I have also made several project on some curricular units, such as creating a basic social network, a quiz, a java application to manage canoeing trials, and some others.

The biggest app that I have ever developed was created in partnership with a company. The app consisted of an application that helped with the recruitment of workers to businesses, allowing a fast and effective process. This app was developed in group (with two other colleagues), which allowed me to acquire a greater competence to work as a team, and nowadays that is a very important asset.

Furthermore, hardware is also a topic that interests me a lot, more specifically connecting sensors to arduino. The best project that I have ever developed was a car whose purpose was to take photos, with a Bluetooth module that connects to a mobile phone. At this moment I am also developing a mechanism that counts canoeing paddles.

In terms of the future I have many expectations and many project in mind. One of my goals would be to develop for web, however, the area of security also captivates me a lot.

I believe that my acquired skills, added to my desire to learn, dynamism, motivation and dedication, make me a good candidate to occupy any position related to my area.

To conclude, I intend to achieve good results and also develop, even more, my competences as well as my knowledge.

Decidi optar por esta licenciatura porque esta sempre foi uma área que me despertou interesse. Esta tem-me permitido entrar no “mundo” da programação através das linguagens lecionadas no curso e das metodologias adquiridas nas aulas. Ou seja, ao longo do meu percurso académico tenho adquirido várias competências que são fundamentais para o meu desenvolvimento e que me habilitam no exercício da profissão de Engenheiro Informático. Competências práticas e cognitivas. Assim, com o auxílio destes conhecimentos adquiridos, sou ainda capaz de, posteriormente ao tempo de aulas, aprender novas linguagens de uma forma autodidata.

Antes da licenciatura pertenci a um fórum em que o foco principal era trabalhar com css e html. Também criei vários blogs durante o ensino secundário que me permitiram testar várias técnicas que se revelaram importantes.

Durante a licenciatura aprendi C, Java, PHP, NodeJS, entre outras tecnologias.

Desenvolvi um site que está a ser muito bem sucedido, e toda a construção, manutenção e dedicação para que o site progredisse cada vez mais fez-me despertar o interesse na investigação por Search Engine Optimization de modo a poder alcançar os primeiros lugares do google.

Também realizei vários projetos para algumas unidades curriculares, como a criação de uma rede social básica, um quiz, uma aplicação em java para gerir provas de canoagem, entre várias outras.

Neste momento também estou a desenvolver um contador de pagaiadas.

Relativamente ao futuro tenho várias expectativas e sempre vários projetos em mente. Um dos meus objetivos seria desenvolver para a web, contudo, a área da segurança também me cativa imenso.

Acredito que as minhas competências adquiridas, somadas à minha vontade de aprendizagem, dinamismo, motivação e dedicação me tornam uma boa opção para ocupar qualquer cargo relacionado com a minha área. Para finalizar, pretendo alcançar bons resultados, desenvolver cada vez mais as minhas competências e conhecimentos.

Arduino is an open source computer hardware and software company, project, and user community that designs and manufactures single-board microcontrollers and microcontroller kits for building digital devices and interactive objects that can sense and control objects in the physical world. The project's products are distributed as open-source hardware and software, which are licensed under the GNU Lesser General Public License (LGPL) or the GNU General Public License (GPL),[1] permitting the manufacture of Arduino boards and software distribution by anyone. Arduino boards are available commercially in preassembled form, or as do-it-yourself kits. (Wikipédia)
